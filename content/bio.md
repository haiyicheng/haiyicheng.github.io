I am an M.A. student in the Educational Innovation, Technology, and Entrepreneurship program at the School of Education, UNC-Chapel Hill. **I will be joining the Department of Sociology, University of Virginia as a PhD student in Fall 2026.**

Prior to this, I obtained a B.A. (*Hons*) in Politics, Economics, and Philosophy from Renmin University of China, and was a summer exchange student at UC Berkeley.

I have broad interests at the intersections of political, cultural, and historical sociology. I am interested in how modern states produce knowledge about society and populations. I am also interested in how social and institutional environments become embedded in people's life chances in ways that accumulate over time.

Outside of academia, I enjoy cooking. I am a big fan of Chinese cuisine (especially Sichuan) and Italian cuisine. I am also learning to play the classical guitar.
